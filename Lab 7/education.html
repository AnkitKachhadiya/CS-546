My Education

University of Helsinki

Currently I am pursuing my Master's in Computer Science from University of Helsinki. I chose this university because the education system in Finland is considered one of the best in the world. An equal school system, high regard for teachers, research, innovation and top-quality teaching have played a significant role in making it a success.

My favorite class is Automated Logical Reasoning.

Meeting Linus Torvalds when he came to University for guest lecture is one of the most memorable memory.

ITMO University

I did my Bachelor's in Computer Science from ITMO University a research institution in Saint Petersburg, Russia. University is known for winning many international competitive programming competitions check here for all the competitions won by the university and its also known its rigorous Computer Science course.

My favourite class was Virtual and Augmented Reality.

Preparing for competitive programming competitions with my friends was fun, exciting and its one of the most memorable memory from that time.
